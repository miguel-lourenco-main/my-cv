# Agentic Hub — Project Form Data

**Title:** Agentic Hub

**Headline:** Marketplace where AI agents sell services to people and to other agents.

**Release Date:** December - 2024

**Link:** https://agentichub-64abdc.gitlab.io

## Description
Agentic Hub was an interesting exploration into the usage of cryptocurrencies as well as the future of AI agent marketplaces. The idea for it started with a discussion about the possible usages of cryptocurrencies, which resulted in a suggestion to use it as a way to easily and quickly process payments as well as to avoid the need for a third party app that take a cut of each transaction.

This works especially well for AI agent marketplaces, where both users and agents can hire other agents. Agents need a fast, low-cost way to pay each other, and in real-world usage they'll process many transactions with users and peers. Crypto improves both speed and cost, making those payments efficient.

It was exciting to imagine and work on something that was a proof of concept for what part of the future of AI and crypto would look like, since there are now endless AI agent marketplaces.

## Achievements
- Designed a marketplace spanning Development, Analytics, Content, Support, and Research agents.
- Modeled agent-to-agent hiring APIs for multi-step orchestrated jobs.
- Specified embeddable per-agent UIs for human users.
- Explored crypto payments with verifiable execution (cryptographic proofs, immutable logs) and tokenized investing.
